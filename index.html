Raising Eternal Stewardships

Relief and Hope to Marginalized Peoples of the World

EE Global Ministries brings help and hope to the world by offering charitable humanitarian aid and relief to the marginalized, vulnerable, and disadvantaged.

  • We focus on children and the poor.
  • We restore the dreams of individuals through support, education and work opportunities.
  • We believe in maximizing our efforts through cooperation with like-minded people.

Empowering Churches To Lead Next Generation Disciples

Global Children's Network has developed into a network of children, youth and family ministry leaders, pastors, and parents from country to country around the world.

  • We provide the amazing ministry platform called Malachi Kids Club which is free to churches around the world.
  • Our network of leaders and children has a wide range of needs to fill.
  • From sponsoring missionaries, to project resources, every cent and dollar is spent towards equipping churches to make next generation disciples.

Seeing Vibrant Communities Of Jesus Followers Among The Least Reached

OM Ships exists to show that Jesus makes the difference in all our lives. We take the message of the gospel to some of the least-reached people and places.

  • OM Ships is a family of dedicated, selfless people whom God has called to offer their abilities and their heart.
  • We depend humbly upon our global support base of faithful partners, whose prayers and finance make our service possible.
  • Since 1970 OM's ships have visited more than 480 different ports in 151 countries and territories and welcomed over 47 million visitors on board.
