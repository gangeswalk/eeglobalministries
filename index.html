Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is graphic design, anyway?

    Graphic design is all about telling a story visually. A graphic designer will use imagery of many kinds to tell a story in a way that supports a brand's message and evokes an emotional response from the observer. Ultimately, our goal is to fulfill our client's desire to communicate an idea in a visually appealing way.

  • How do I submit my ideas for the work I would like done?

    We can receive sketches, images, diagrams, text, ideas online. Send us an email in the link below and we can arrange for this. We can also meet in person to discuss your project and look at options. Meeting in a coffee shop is a great way to get started!
